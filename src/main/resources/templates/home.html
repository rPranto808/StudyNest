Frequently Asked Questions

What is Skillshare?
Skillshare is an online learning platform with thousands of classes on various topics to help people build skills.
What is included in my Skillshare membership?
A Skillshare membership gives you unlimited access to classes on various subjects including design, business, and more.
What can I learn from Skillshare?
Skillshare offers classes in creative fields like illustration, photography, and writing, as well as business and lifestyle skills.
What happens after my trial is over?
After your trial ends, you can choose to continue with a paid membership to keep learning on Skillshare.
Can I teach on Skillshare?
Yes, Skillshare allows creators and educators to teach classes and share their expertise with learners worldwide.
